Fonema service with Odoo
Servicio de Fonema con Odoo

When receiving a call you will be able to see the name of the contact, access their Odoo contact, as well as keep a record of the contact's incoming and outgoing calls by storing it in your chatter (contact history).

🗣 Al recibir una llamada, podrá ver el nombre del contacto, acceder a su contacto de Odoo, así como mantener un registro de las llamadas entrantes y salientes del contacto almacenándolo en su chat (historial de contactos).

To use this module, you need an account with Monema. You can create an account by visiting this page: https://www.monema.es/en/try-our-virtual-pbx-for-free/

🗣 Para usar este módulo, necesita una cuenta con Monema. Puede crear una cuenta visitando esta página: https://www.monema.es/probar-centralita-virtual/

What are the advantages of Fonema integration with Odoo?
¿Cuáles son las ventajas de la integración de Fonema con Odoo?

✓ Caller ID / ID de llamada

By activating the integration with Odoo, every time you receive a call the caller's phone number will be searched among the Odoo contacts.

If you receive the call on your computer with softphone or physical IP phone, you will be able to see the name of the associated contact.

And on your mobile, with the Fonema app, you will receive a PUSH notification with the name of the contact.

✓ Call annotation / Anotación de llamadas

View the call log from the chatter (contact history) of the contact.

✓ Access to contact / Acceso a la ficha del contacto

If you have the Fonema Chrome extension installed, you can access the Odoo contact as soon as you pick up an incoming call from the contact and at the time you make a call to him.
